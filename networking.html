Networking and Connections

Build Meaningful Professional Relationships

Networking is a crucial skill for career development, allowing you to connect with professionals in your field, learn about industry trends, and discover job opportunities. Whether you're new to networking or looking to enhance your existing skills, these resources will guide you through the process of building and maintaining valuable connections.

UMSI students have access to a wide range of networking opportunities, from alumni events to industry meetups. Use this page to find tips, strategies, and tools to help you make the most of these connections.

Networking Strategies: Steps to Success

  1. Identify Your Goals: Determine what you want to achieve through networking, whether it's finding a mentor, learning about a specific industry, or discovering job opportunities.
  2. Research Contacts: Use LinkedIn, alumni networks, and professional organizations to find individuals who align with your goals.
  3. Prepare Your Introduction: Craft a concise and engaging elevator pitch that highlights your background and interests.
  4. Engage in Conversations: Attend events, join online forums, and participate in discussions to connect with professionals.
  5. Follow Up: Send personalized thank-you notes or messages after meetings to express your appreciation and keep the conversation going.
  6. Maintain Relationships: Regularly check in with your contacts, share relevant articles or updates, and offer assistance when possible.

Make the Most of UMSI Career Fairs

Career fairs are a great way to connect directly with employers. Prepare by researching companies, practicing your pitch, and bringing polished resumes.

Strengthen Your Online Presence

  • Build a strong LinkedIn profile that highlights your skills and experiences.
  • Engage with UMSI alumni groups and recruiters online.
  • Share insights and projects to showcase your expertise.

Networking Resources

  • LinkedIn: Create a professional profile and connect with industry professionals.
  • Handshake: Access job postings and networking events specifically for UMSI students.
  • Meetup: Find local groups and events related to your field of interest.
  • Eventbrite: Discover professional events and conferences.
  • The Muse: Networking Tips: Articles and advice on effective networking strategies.
